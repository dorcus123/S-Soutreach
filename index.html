Mission

Spirit & Soul Outreach exists as a response to the love, mercy, and restoration I personally received from God and through the community of first mennonite church of San Francisco Church. Having been carried, supported, and uplifted during seasons of need, my heart has been shaped by compassion and gratitude and a deep responsibility to give that love away.

This outreach also flows from a calling I have walked in quietly for many years, serving, interceding, and helping where I could often alone, often unseen. Now, in this season, I sense God inviting others to walk alongside me, so that together we can extend hope, dignity, and practical support to widows, single mothers, and vulnerable children in Africa. Spirit & Soul Outreach is not about charity alone it is about shared compassion, obedience to God‚Äôs leading, and the belief that love multiplies when it is given freely. We welcome all who feel stirred to pray, to serve, or to give, as partners in helping restore lives and remind those in need that they are seen, loved, and not forgotten.

Case #2: ORPHAN

Need: Medical Care,school fees & Shelter

Hamida is a brave young girl currently under the compassionate care of Catherine, a trusted friend of her late mother. When Hamida's mother passed away(For HIV AIDS), Catherine her mother's distant friend stepped forward in love to care for her, despite having no legal or family obligation to do so. Catherine now carries the full responsibility of providing for Hamida's daily needs. This includes ensuring she has food, clothing, access to education, and consistent medical care. Due to limited resources, meeting these needs has become increasingly difficult. Hamida depends on regular medical treatment and ongoing support, which places a heavy financial and emotional burden on her caregiver. Catherine does her best, but she cannot do this alone. Through Spirit & Soul Outreach, we are prayerfully seeking support to help cover Hamida's essential needsmedical care, nutrition, clothing, and school related expenses so that she can grow in stability, health, and hope.

  • Shelter placement
  • toys/clothes
